MIDI Floppy Drives

Got old floppy drives or hard drives laying around and don't want to throw them away? This is the project for you! In the fall of 2017, my English group made a project which uses both of these old drives and produces a unique musical sound via an Arduino Mega as a controller! We detail how to replicate Moppy and describe our discoveries and efforts extending this application to better support live playback using a MIDI keyboard. While the video displayed is not our presentation, it demonstrates what our project was able to do.
